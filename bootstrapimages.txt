Bootstrap Images:

img class rounded:
The .img-rounded class adds rounded corners to an image (not available in IE8):
class="img-rounded"

img class circled:
Adds circle corners
class="img-circle"

img class thumbnail:
img-thumbnail class shapes the image to a thumbnail:
class="img-thumbnail"

Responsive images:
Responsive images automatically adjust to fit the size of the screen.
img-responsive class applies display: block; and max-width: 100%; and height: auto; to the image:
class="img-responsive"

Responsive Embed:
Emeds a video
<!-- 16:9 aspect ratio -->
<!-- 4:3 aspect ratio -->
<div class="embed-responsive embed-responsive-16by9">
<iframe class="embed-responsive-item" 
src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/MbCEBY17Lw8"></iframe>
video aspect ratios are 4:3 (the universal video format of the 20th century), and 16:9 (universal for HD television and European digital television).
